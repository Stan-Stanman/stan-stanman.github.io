Design of a plastic blood pump housing under cyclic load

As part of my exam “Case studies - Applied FEM”, I have planned to work on the case study of a blood pump housing. I will work on a simplified version of a real 3D model by TRIBECRAFT. This project will include:

  • Setting of FE models in Ansys for 1 working cycle with particular concern to material properties and contacts.
  • Identification of the stress amplitude and mean stress to assess fatigue strength.
