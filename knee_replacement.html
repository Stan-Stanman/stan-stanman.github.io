Biomechanical Assessment of Total Knee Replacement (TKR) Tibial Insert via FEA

As part of my exam “Case studies - Applied FEM”, I'm working on the case study of a knee prosthesis. This includes:

  • Selecting a representative loading and kinematic profile based on the testing method described in the ISO 12423-3 standard and the already existing literature on this topic.
  • Setup of FE models in Ansys following an engineering approach (from linear to nonlinear models).
  • Proposing a design improvement based mainly on the stress field and the contact pressure of the UHMWPE insert.
